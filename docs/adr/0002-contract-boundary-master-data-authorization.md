# Contract boundary with master-data authorization

Opening-condition personnel and equipment checks use contracts and supplemental basis records to establish the participating organization and work scope, then use published or human-approved project master data to authorize specific personnel and equipment. The platform does not require every worker or device to be enumerated in the contract text, because real construction contracts often define responsibility boundaries rather than complete operational rosters.

## Considered Options

- Require all passable personnel and equipment to be explicitly listed in the contract or attachment.
- Allow any uploaded personnel or equipment material to pass if it appears relevant.
- Use contract boundary plus confirmed project master data.

## Consequences

Personnel and equipment cannot pass from arbitrary uploaded files alone, but the pilot remains realistic for subcontracting, rental equipment, and dynamically updated construction teams.
